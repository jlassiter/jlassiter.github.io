About Me

I grew up in the south of the United States. My family and I moved a bit between Georgia and the Carolinas.

I migrated to New York City in 1999. I love the food, energy, and opportunites of NYC, but above all, I love the diversity of people and cultures. It is a pleasure to get to know people of various backgrounds. Understanding how they think and solve problems helps me improve each day.

When I'm not solving problems, I enjoy learning about new technologies and expanding my knowledge of coding. I also enjoy spending time with my family, including my loving dogs, and traveling as much as possible.
